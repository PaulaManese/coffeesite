ABOUT THE CAFE

Our Heritage

Our story begins in 2021 along the busy streets of Cheonggyecheon-ro, Seoul, South Korea. It was here where Paowiseu Keopi opened its first store, offering fresh-roasted coffee beans, tea and spices from around the world for our customers to take home. Our name was inspired by the owner, Paowii.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Paula Manese

Opening hours: Everyday from 6am to 9pm.
